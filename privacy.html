Privacy notice

This covers this website and the sqTile newsletter. The sqTile app itself is covered by its own app privacy policy.

Who is responsible

The website

This site is static — there is no server of mine, no database, no accounts and no analytics. Nothing you do here is tracked or profiled.

  • Hosting. The pages are served by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). Like any web server, it processes the requests it receives, including your IP address, for delivery and security. See GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Cookies. None. Your light/dark choice is kept in your browser's local storage, on your device only — it is never sent anywhere.
  • Blog posts. Post pages load two open-source libraries (for Markdown and code highlighting) from the jsDelivr CDN, which means your browser makes a request to cdn.jsdelivr.net and that service sees your IP address. See jsDelivr's privacy policy.

The newsletter

If you subscribe, the only thing collected is your email address. It is used for one purpose: sending you notes about sqTile releases.

  • Legal basis. Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given by ticking the box and confirming the link in the email that follows. You can withdraw it at any time — that does not affect anything sent before.
  • Processor. The list is managed by MailerLite (UAB MailerLite, Lithuania), which stores your address and records whether the emails were delivered and opened. See MailerLite's privacy policy.
  • How long. Until you unsubscribe. Every email has a one-click unsubscribe link; after that your address is removed from the list.
  • Never. The list is not sold, rented, shared or used for anything other than sqTile updates, and there is no advertising in it.

Your rights

Write to contact@gradomski.dev and I'll handle it. You also have the right to complain to the Polish supervisory authority, the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO), ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warsaw.
